## Intent (one paragraph)

The operator's landing dashboard after sign-in — a "Welcome back" overview surfacing
headline KPIs (transactions, average order value, GMV), integration health, recent
activity, exceptions/actions required, and sales-by-store at a glance. It is the entry
point that orients an operator before they drill into Sales, Inventory, or Monitoring.
