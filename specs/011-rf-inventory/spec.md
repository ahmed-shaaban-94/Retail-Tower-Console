## Intent (one paragraph)

An inventory overview across stores: stock levels, availability and stock-coverage
indicators, top/low-stock items, value on hand, and per-product detail. It gives an
operator visibility into what is in stock, where, and what needs attention
(reorder / out-of-stock / overstock).
