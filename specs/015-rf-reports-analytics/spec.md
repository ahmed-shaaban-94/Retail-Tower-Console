## Intent (one paragraph)

A reports-and-analytics screen: aggregate trend charts (sales over time, distribution
breakdowns), comparative bar/line/donut visualizations, and a tabular report with
date-range filtering. It is the deeper analytical layer above the at-a-glance Overview
and Sales screens, oriented to reporting rather than live operations.
